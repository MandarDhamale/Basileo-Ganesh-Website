About the Festival

The Basileo Society Ganesh Festival is an annual event that brings together the community to celebrate Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles and the god of new beginnings. Our festival is a joyous occasion filled with music, dance, and devotion.

Cultural Programs

Enjoy a variety of cultural performances by local artists.

Visarjan

Participate in the grand visarjan procession of Lord Ganesha.
